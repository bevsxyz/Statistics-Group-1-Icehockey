icehockey

Executive Summary

Our analysis involves the single-year data of the 2023 Women's Ice Hockey World Championship. We will address the individual player performance with linear regression and try to identify the key attributes affecting the performance..

Problem Statement

Comprehensive sports analytics are essential for teams and athletes in order to make data-informed decisions and optimize performance. Furthermore, a rich source of information can significantly boost fan loyalty. In women’s ice hockey, the publicly available data is limited in how important the attribute is for the player’s performance. Moreover, a tidy dataset is not currently available. Hence, we wish to examine the player statistics of the participants in the 2023 Women’s Ice Hockey World Championship and identify the key performance indicators.

Proposed Solution

Using publicly available data from the 2023 Women’s Ice Hockey World Championship, we will compile a tidy dataset. Furthermore, we will perform regression analysis to identify the dependence of the individual variables on the player’s performance. We expect to derive critical insights that could have implications for team strategizing. The insights will also help ice hockey fans understand their favorite players better.
